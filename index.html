May Is Celiac Disease Awareness Month!

Celiac and Gluten-Free Fast Facts: 

      • The latest research estimates that as many as one in 133 Americans, or about 1% of the population, has celiac disease.
      • It is estimated that 83% of Americans who have celiac disease are undiagnosed or misdiagnosed with other conditions.
      • 6-10 years is the average time a person waits to be correctly diagnosed.
      • Celiac disease can lead to a number of other disorders including infertility, reduced bone density, neurological disorders, some cancers, and other autoimmune diseases.
      • There are NO pharmaceutical cures for celiac disease.
      • A 100% gluten-free diet is the only existing treatment for celiac today.
